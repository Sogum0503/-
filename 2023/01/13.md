## 프로그래머스 
### 입양 시각 구하기(1)
```sql
SELECT HOUR(DATETIME) AS HOUR,
        COUNT(*)
FROM ANIMAL_OUTS
WHERE HOUR(DATETIME) >= 9 AND HOUR(DATETIME) <= 19
GROUP BY 1
ORDER BY 1 
```

### DATETIME에서 DATE로 형 변환
```sql
SELECT ANIMAL_ID,
        NAME,
        DATE_FORMAT(DATETIME,'%Y-%m-%d') AS "날짜 "
FROM ANIMAL_INS 
ORDER BY ANIMAL_ID 
```
