### LEETCODE 문제풀이 
#### [2041#_MEDIUM] Accepted Candidates From the Interviews
쿼리 
```sql
select candidate_id 
from candidates c 
    left join rounds r on c.interview_id = r.interview_id 
where years_of_exp >= 2 
group by candidate_id 
having sum(score) > 15
```
