### LEETCODE 문제풀이

#### [2159#_MEDIUM] Order Two Columns Independently
최종 쿼리
```sql
with f_st as (
    select first_col,
            row_number() over(order by first_col asc) as rnk
    from data),
    s_cn as(
    select second_col,
            row_number() over(order by second_col desc) as rnk
    from data)
    
select first_col,
        second_col
from f_st  
    inner join s_cn on f_st.rnk = s_cn.rnk
```
