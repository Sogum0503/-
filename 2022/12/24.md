### 1565. Unique Orders and Customers Per Month
```sql
SELECT DATE_FORMAT(order_date, '%Y-%m') AS month,
     COUNT(order_id) AS order_count,
     COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) AS customer_count  
FROM Orders 
WHERE invoice > 20 
GROUP BY month
```
