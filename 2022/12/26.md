## 프로그래머스
### 가장 비싼 상품 구하기
```sql
SELECT MAX(PRICE) AS MAX_PRICE
FROM PRODUCT
```
### 조건에 맞는 회원수 구하기
```sql
SELECT COUNT(*)
FROM USER_INFO
WHERE YEAR(JOINED) = 2021
AND AGE >= 20 
AND AGE <= 29
```

### 상품 별 오프라인 매출 구하기
```SQL
SELECT PRODUCT_CODE,
        SUM(PRICE*SALES_AMOUNT) AS SALES
FROM PRODUCT P
    JOIN OFFLINE_SALE O ON P.PRODUCT_ID = O.PRODUCT_ID  
GROUP BY PRODUCT_CODE
ORDER BY SALES DESC, PRODUCT_CODE ASC
```
