## leetcode 
### 1741. Find Total Time Spent by Each Employee
```sql
SELECT event_day AS day,
        emp_id,
        SUM(out_time - in_time) AS total_time
FROM Employees 
GROUP BY day, emp_id  
```

### 1757. Recyclable and Low Fat Products
```sql
SELECT product_id  
FROM Products 
WHERE low_fats = 'Y' AND recyclable = 'Y'
```
